Molecular Science Corporation (MSC) provides product development, formulations and testing, terpene and cannabis genetics, commercial research and value-added services to the regulated cannabis sector.

MSC is using our advanced knowledge in the production and distribution of cannabis based products for the consumer market. Guided by experienced business executives, we are proud to have some of the most extensive and recognized leaders in the pharmaceutical, plant, cannabinoid science associated with our company.

Product Development & Sales

MSC develops, formulates and creates consumer products and cannabis formulations for distribution through established regulated networks. Our formulations are backed by extensive commercial research and designed for maximum efficacy. MSC will also distribute cannabis products utilizing its formulations upon licensing.
